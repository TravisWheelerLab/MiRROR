The .msf files in PXD004325 are relational databases. Tables MassPeaks, PeptideScores and PeptideScores_decoy, Peptides (and related tables, and again for Proteins rather than Peptides), SpectraHeaders collectively about the same thing as an msms.txt.

PXD004424 [inpr]
The .xml files in PXD004424 are Mascot search results formatted according to 
http://www.matrixscience.com/xmlns/schema/mascot_search_results_2/#ns_msr.
Since we have one .xml for every .mgf, we can more or less reconstruct peptide sequence to spectrum relationships. The .mgf are relatively small, though, compared to quite a few half-gig .raw files. Not sure what's going on, the paper alludes to maybe combining portions of the .raw files. Iterating the .mgf using Pyteomics yields standard spectra data.
